Outreach Programs

Serving Our Community with Love and Compassion

Making a positive impact through practical service, community engagement, and genuine care for those in need

Our Mission to Serve

Grace Fellowship Church is committed to making a positive impact in our community through various outreach programs. We believe in serving others as Christ served us, reaching out to those in need with practical help, spiritual support, and genuine care.

Our Outreach Mission

To demonstrate God's love through practical service, community engagement, and compassionate outreach to those in need, both locally and globally.

Current Outreach Initiatives

Community Food Pantry

Schedule: Every Saturday, 9:00 AM - 12:00 PM

Church Fellowship Hall

Providing groceries and essential supplies to families facing food insecurity in our community.

Homeless Outreach Ministry

Schedule: First Saturday of each month, 6:00 PM - 9:00 PM

Downtown Area

Monthly street outreach providing meals, clothing, hygiene kits, and prayer to our homeless neighbors.

Prison Ministry

Schedule: Weekly visits on Thursdays, 6:00 PM - 8:00 PM

County Correctional Facility

Bringing hope and spiritual support to incarcerated individuals through Bible studies, counseling, and reentry assistance.

Senior Care & Companionship

Schedule: Tuesdays and Thursdays, 2:00 PM - 4:00 PM

Various Nursing Homes

Visiting elderly community members in nursing homes and providing companionship, assistance, and spiritual encouragement.

Refugee Resettlement Support

Schedule: Ongoing support with weekly meetings on Wednesdays, 7:00 PM

Church Community Center

Helping refugee families integrate into our community by providing housing assistance, job training, English classes, and cultural orientation.

Community Garden Project

Schedule: Spring through Fall, Saturdays 8:00 AM - 11:00 AM

Church Property - West Side

Growing fresh produce for our food pantry and teaching sustainable gardening practices to community members.

Special Annual Programs

Back to School Drive

August (Annual Event) Church Campus

Annual program providing school supplies, backpacks, and clothing to children from low-income families.

Medical Mission Trips

Summer (June-July) and Winter (January-February) International Destinations

Annual international mission trips providing medical care, health education, and spiritual ministry to underserved communities.

Impact Stories

From Homelessness to Hope

Through our homeless outreach ministry, we met James, who had been living on the streets for two years. With consistent support, job training, and spiritual guidance, James secured stable housing and employment. Today, he volunteers with our outreach team, helping others find the same hope he discovered.

A Family's Fresh Start

The Ahmed family arrived as refugees with nothing but hope for a better future. Our refugee support program helped them find housing, learn English, and navigate their new community. Now, two years later, both parents are employed, their children are thriving in school, and they're active members of our church family.

Healing Through Service

Sarah joined our prison ministry after her own brother was incarcerated. Through weekly visits and Bible studies, she's witnessed incredible transformations. Several former inmates she mentored are now productive members of society, and three have joined our church community.

Volunteer Opportunities

Food Pantry Volunteer

Time Commitment: 4 hours per month (one Saturday)

Help sort, organize, and distribute food to families in need. No experience necessary, just a willing heart to serve.

Homeless Outreach Team Member

Time Commitment: One evening per month (first Saturday, 6-9 PM)

Join our team in serving meals, distributing supplies, and building relationships with our homeless neighbors.

Prison Ministry Mentor

Time Commitment: Weekly visits (Thursdays, 6-8 PM)

Provide spiritual guidance, Bible study leadership, and reentry support to incarcerated individuals.

Senior Companion Visitor

Time Commitment: 2-4 hours per week

Brighten the day of elderly residents through visits, conversation, activities, and prayer.

English Language Tutor

Time Commitment: 2 hours per week

Help refugee families learn English and navigate American culture through one-on-one or small group tutoring.

Community Garden Volunteer

Time Commitment: 3 hours per week (Saturdays, 8-11 AM)

Help grow fresh produce for our food pantry while learning sustainable gardening practices.
