Children's Ministry

Nurturing Young Hearts for Jesus

Birth through 5th Grade

Welcome to Children's Ministry

Our Children's Ministry is a vibrant, safe, and fun place where kids from birth through 5th grade can learn about God's love in age-appropriate ways. Through engaging Bible stories, creative activities, worship songs, and caring teachers, we help children build a strong foundation of faith while making lasting friendships.

Our Mission

To partner with families in raising children who know, love, and follow Jesus Christ through engaging, age-appropriate teaching and activities in a safe, nurturing environment.

Safety & Security

Background Checks

All volunteers and staff complete comprehensive background checks before serving with children.

Two-Adult Rule

At least two screened adults are present in every classroom at all times.

Check-In System

Secure electronic check-in/check-out system with matching ID tags for parents and children.

Allergy Awareness

We track and accommodate food allergies and medical needs for each child.

Clean Environment

Regular cleaning and sanitization of all toys, surfaces, and materials.

Emergency Procedures

Staff trained in emergency response, first aid, and evacuation procedures.
