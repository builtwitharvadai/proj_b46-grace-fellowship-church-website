## Finding Images on Unsplash

### Getting Started with Unsplash

1. **Visit Unsplash**: Go to [https://unsplash.com](https://unsplash.com)
2. **Search for Images**: Use the search bar to find relevant images
3. **Free to Use**: All Unsplash images are free for commercial and non-commercial use

### Search Tips for Church Content

**Recommended Search Terms:**
- `church community`
- `worship hands`
- `prayer group`
- `bible study`
- `children ministry`
- `youth group`
- `community service`
- `helping hands`
- `family worship`
- `church interior`

**Quality Criteria:**
- High resolution (minimum 1920px width)
- Good lighting and composition
- Authentic, candid moments
- Diverse representation
- Professional quality

### Selecting the Right Image

**Consider:**
- **Relevance**: Does it match the content?
- **Emotion**: Does it convey the right feeling?
- **Diversity**: Does it represent our inclusive community?
- **Quality**: Is it sharp and well-lit?
- **Composition**: Is the subject clear and focused?
